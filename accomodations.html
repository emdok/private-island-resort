Accomodations

Turtle Island Fiji is a stunning, 500-acre privately owned island—the ultimate vacation resort perfect for honeymoons, anniversaries, weddings, vow renewals or even to rent your own private island. We accommodate just 28 guests at a time. Each of our 14 bures (exquisite hand-crafted Fijian villas) are nestled along the famous “Blue Lagoon” and are separated from each other by generous expanses, meaning you’ll have your own slice of exotic island paradise.

10 QUICK FACTS ABOUT TURTLE ISLAND

  • 500 acres, located in the Yasawas in Fiji

  • Privately owned, giving you complete run of the island

  • 14 light-filled bures (Fijian villas) situated on the glistening Blue Lagoon

  • Numerous white beaches fringe the island, each appointed with beach bure and hammock for relaxing solitude

  • Exquisite wines paired with Pacific-Rim cuisine prepared from just-caught seafood and 5 acres of lovingly tended gardens

  • Plenty of time for play with water sports or hikes

  • Spa to revive your body, and your soul

  • Thriving natural environment, with power harvested from our solar farm

  • Cultural haven for the Fijian way of life, centered in ancient values of respect and joy

  • The perfect place to be in love, or fall in love all over again
